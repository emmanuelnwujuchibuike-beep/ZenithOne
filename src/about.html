Our Mission

Banking the Way It
Was Meant to Be

ZenithOne was founded on a single, enduring premise: that the relationship between a bank and its client should be built on unwavering trust, deep expertise, and a genuine commitment to the client's wellbeing — not the bank's profitability.

Today, that founding principle guides every decision we make, from the products we offer to the advisors we hire to the technology we build.

"A bank should exist to serve its clients. Every other consideration — profit, growth, reputation — follows from that." — Elias Zenith, Founder, 1887

Our History

137 Years of
Milestones

From a single office on Wall Street to a nationwide presence — our history is one of steady, principled growth, never chasing trends, always serving clients.

1887
Founded by Elias Zenith
Established at 14 Wall Street, New York City, with $2 million in founding capital. The bank's charter stated its purpose plainly: "to serve the financial needs of individuals and families with honesty and excellence."
1929
Survived the Great Crash
While hundreds of banks failed in 1929 and 1930, ZenithOne remained solvent — owing to its conservative lending practices, modest leverage, and deep client relationships that prevented bank runs.
1968
National Expansion
Opened offices in Boston, Chicago, and San Francisco — the first steps in becoming a truly national private bank, while maintaining the boutique client experience that defines ZenithOne.
1995
Wealth Management Launch
Formally established the ZenithOne Wealth Management division, bringing institutional investment capabilities to private clients for the first time and crossing $1 billion in AUM within 18 months.
2018
Digital Transformation
Launched ZenithOne Digital — a proprietary banking platform combining AI-powered insights with the personalized service our clients have always known. Rated #1 private bank app by Financial Times readers.
2024
$48B in Assets, 137 Years Strong
Today, ZenithOne manages $48 billion in client assets across 32 offices, serving 4,800+ private banking and wealth management clients — with the same founding commitment: clients first, always.

Integrity

◆
Excellence
We hire the best, train them rigorously, and hold ourselves to a standard that is simply higher than our competition. Excellence is not a goal — it is a baseline expectation.
